What is Gmeez?

GMEEZ is the first gaming social hub that offers an innovative and complete ecosystem in a single platform that is fully automated and managed by blockchain where anyone can easily socialize, play, interact, capitalize as well as increase their brand and product visibility.

It is the first blockchain-based social gaming hub, accessible worldwide. We connect everything you need to enjoy the challenge of video game competitions.
